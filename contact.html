About the studio

11h21.studio is a minimal web design studio working with independent brands, creative practices and small businesses worldwide. I handle web design and build across Shopify, WordPress, Squarespace, Readymag and hand-coded HTML; as well as SEO and GEO strategy, copywriting, DTP design and social content creation.

I am currently based in The Hague, Netherlands, with South Africa as my next base. All work is delivered remotely; communication is direct and personal throughout. No account managers, no discovery decks, no unnecessary process.

11h21.studio is a sister project of 11h21.com, the photographic studio and fine art print imprint of Alfred Lor.
