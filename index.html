What we do

Web Design & Build

Clean, functional websites for independent projects and creative businesses. I work across Shopify, WordPress, Squarespace, Readymag and hand-coded HTML; choosing the right tool for each brief, not the easiest one for us.

Fast turnaround, direct access, no agency overhead. For clients who need to launch before the moment passes, I offer guerrilla-style HTML builds that go live in days and cost a fraction of a traditional agency quote.

CMS Migration & Upkeep

Already on a platform that no longer serves you? I relocate, rebuild and relaunch; carrying across your content, improving your structure, making sure nothing gets lost in the move. Every migration is an opportunity to fix the things that were never quite right the first time.

Ongoing upkeep packages are available across all platforms; so your site stays current, secure and performing without you having to think about it. One message and it is handled.

SEO, GEO & Content

Strategy-led SEO and AI-era GEO copywriting for independent brands. I work at every level; keyword strategy, heading architecture, meta optimisation, Schema.org structured data, multilingual copy in English and Dutch; and I deploy everything directly into your CMS.

DTP design, social content creation and project management; all shaped by the same minimal, considered aesthetic as my main studio, 11h21.
